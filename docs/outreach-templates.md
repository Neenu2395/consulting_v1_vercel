## 1. HARO / Connectively / Qwoted responses

Sign up at connectively.us (formerly HARO) and qwoted.com as a **source**.
Search or filter for: MBA, business school, graduate school, admissions,
higher education, careers, GMAT. Respond within 2-3 hours of a query when
possible; speed matters more than polish.

and we advise applicants to M7 and top European MBA and Master's programs.

On your question: [2-4 sentences answering their EXACT question with one
specific, non-obvious insight. No pitching. Give the quote they can print.]

Happy to expand on any of this or provide additional comments on deadline.

[Name]
Co-founder, Elite Admissions Consulting
https://elite-admissions-consulting.com
LinkedIn: [personal profile URL]

### Template B: data-free but experience-backed angle

Use when the query asks for stats you don't have. Reframe with firsthand
experience instead:

"I can't speak to industry-wide numbers, but from working directly with
[type of applicants], here is what we consistently see: [insight]."

### HARO rules of thumb
- Answer the exact question asked, in quotable full sentences.
- One insight beats five generic tips.
- Include credentials in one line, never a sales pitch.
- If they use your quote, you typically get a link or a mention. Track which
  outlets respond and prioritize them next time.
